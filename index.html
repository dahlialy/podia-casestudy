Frequently Asked Questions

Can I start building my website and blog for free?

Yep, you can! Podia's 30-day free trial includes our no-code website builder and blogging tool so you can set up your full website, blog, and unlimited landing pages. Whether you’re a new creator or a seasoned small business owner, we make it easy and affordable to establish a memorable online presence.

Can I build landing pages in Podia?

Absolutely! You can use the Podia website tool to create a full website, stand-alone landing pages, or both. You can also decide whether or not each page has a site header. No matter what you create, you’ll have access to the same great library of website sections and design features.

Does Podia include website hosting?

We host all Podia websites, landing pages, and blogs, so you don’t have to worry about finding, maintaining, and paying for your own hosting. Podia websites also come with an SSL certificate to keep your site safe and secure.

Can I connect a custom domain?

All plans come with a free podia.com subdomain that you can use for as long as you like. If you prefer to use a custom domain name, you can connect your custom domain on Mover and Shaker.

Does Podia have SEO features?

Podia has SEO features for your website pages, landing pages, and blog posts. For each page and post, you can adjust your SEO title and description, customize your URL, and decide whether or not your content should appear in the search results.

What features does Podia have?

Podia is an all-in-one platform for running your online business. You get a full website, your own blog, unlimited landing pages, and the ability to sell digital products like courses, coaching, downloads, webinars, and any other type of file you like. Podia also includes your checkout cart, online store, affiliates, and email marketing so you don’t have to connect other tools.
